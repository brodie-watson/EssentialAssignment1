Interest in IT:

My interest first began as a boy growing up on a farm in Cairns. My friend's older brothers had, what was at the time, high-tech computers. They would explain to me in detail about how the hardware worked, why it made the computer run smoothly, what viruses were, and how to care for the computer. Over time, media such as The Matrix and other movies were released, which sparked my interest in programming.

During high school I took as many computer classes as I could. During this time I learned BASIC programming, and robotics. We also took ethics in IT. After high school, I managed to land a job as a lead web developer for a company that produced software for real estate agents. During this time I learned project management, as well as Git skills, DNS management, and domain maintenance.

I decided to enrol online at RMIT after deciding to engage in online university, and after looking through the entire course syllabus, found that the Bachelor of IT would produce the skill necessary to become a full stack developer. I was especially interested in the ability to learn Advanced Programming techniques and learn the .NET framework, as there are many jobs available in this domain.
